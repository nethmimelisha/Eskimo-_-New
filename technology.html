DYEING & WINDING PROCESS

Dyeing

Our dye house has the capacity to dye our own yarns and piece goods. The machinery includes:

  • Milnor for piece goods dyeing and washing
  • Scholl and Then hand and cheese dyeing machines
  • Paddle Dyeing Machines / Milling Machine
  • Colour Combi Machine

Small to large lots can be dyed from 5 kg to 250 kg. The laboratory is equipped with sample dyeing machines and testing facilities.

Winding

We operate a Winding Section with various winding and reeling machines. Yarns are waxed or oiled and tested before knitting.

CMC STOLL MACHINE

CMS Stoll is a fully computerized knitting machine capable of handling all Universal Flat Knitting structures including advanced features such as Aran, Cable, Ajour and multicolored Jacquard knitting. Ideal for caps, beanies, scarves, gloves and more.


KNITTING MACHINES FOR HOSIERY PRODUCTS

MATEC MACHINE

This is one of the oldest machines in the hosiery line. It can manufacture socks, tights and leggings with 14gg knitting capability.

SOOSAN MACHINE

Equipped with modern hosiery knitting technology including jacquard and multicolor knitting with up to 5 colors. Commonly used for socks, tights and leggings. 12gg capable.

LONATI MACHINE

LONATI machines feature 14gg knitting and are ideal for manufacturing high-quality socks, tights and leggings.




KNITTING

Knitting is one of the most versatile areas of Eskimo. We have given a great deal of prominence in this century old industry which Eskimo has marked its identity in the world with. We use the latest technology in manufacturing knitted articles and accessories, and it is being regularly updated to stay competitive. We have invested in fully computerized knitting machines in order to enhance the quality of the product and productivity.

DC KNITTING

This is one of the oldest machines in the factory. In the Glove manifacturing process, cuff and hand part could be knitted on this machine. Main yarn types used are Cotton, Acrylic and Wool.

FTK - FINGER THUMB KNITTING

This too is one of the oldest machines in the Factory. This machine is capable of knitting fingers and thumbs to the cuff of a glove.

KOEPER MACHINE

A complete glove could be knitted on this machine. This is capable of knitting stripe patterns with 10 colours.

SHIMA SEIKI GLOVE MACHINE

Complete glove could be knitted on this machine. The specialty in this machine is the capability of knitting Thumb on palm and Thumb on the side.

WARP KNITTING

Only warp knitting structures could be knitted on this machine. The multi coloured fabric could be knitted on warp knitted machin to manufacturing caps & Scarves.

UNIVERSAL FLAT KNITTING MACHINES

Knitting structures such as All knit/Plain knit/Half cardi gan/Full cardigan/Tubular / Half round and rib structures could be knitted on this machine. Six colours could be accommodated and caps and scarves and headbands are knitted in these machines.




CUTTING & SEWING

Process of Cutting

Knitted and woven fabrics are hand and band knife cut. Dye cutting is available when precision cutting of small parts is required.

Modern Technologies for Cutting

We use state of the art Gerber Technology, namely the Gerber Accomack software, to develop patterns, grading of patterns and marker making for the designs created in-house as well as customers' designs. The GTxL Gerber Cutter is the latest addition to outstate the art manufacturing process.

Sewing Process

The sewing Department receives material from Stores as well as Cutting and Milling departments for its produc tion process. Also, there is a sample/ prototype devel opment section. This department has the following array of machines.

Juki single needle lock-stitch
Juki 3,4,5 thread over lock
Various types of single and double needle chain stitch machines
Leather, rubber, piping and blind hem machines 7 Doubles and triple needle flat-lock machines

Special Machine Types

Kansai picot machines.
Albinporket hunting machines.
eyelet hole machines.
Linking machines for knitted caps & shawls.


HAND WORK AND LOOPING, EMBROIDERY, PRINT & HEAT EMBOSS To add more elegance to products Eskimo uses hand work and looping, machine embroidery and prints.
